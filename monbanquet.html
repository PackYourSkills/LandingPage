Shoot and edit videos about French food artisans

The mission you will help us in !

Mon Banquet was created in 2016 to promote local know-how while feeding and treating as many guests as possible for the events’ organizers.

Since its beginnings, Monbanquet has been a caterer a bit special ! We only work with French food, and our buffet comes straight out of their imagination. Bakeries, Cheese makers, wine producers: the products are made the day of the order. They are therefore guaranteed fresh... and of course, gourmet !

We need a videographer

to shoot and edit videos of French food artisans to highlight their know-how as well as videos of some events for our communication

We are working with local French food artisans who all have their own specialties. We would like to have some videos of them in their kitchen and shops. The aim of these video would be to value their skills and make everybody want to try what they are doing. We can also do some portrait to present them.
You will also have the occasion to come with us at some buffets to record how it is during an event.
To finish, we speak easily English, French in the team !

Where you will work

At the heart of a neighbourhood full of great bars and restaurants, we work in a new, spacious and bright office. There is a sofa corner for meetings (or naps), and a sunny terrace for outdoor lunch or evening drinks. Besides if you feel like a Valérie Damidot (famous French decorator on tv), you can even bring your own touch to our nascent decoration.

Where you will live

You will sleep at Maxime’s apartment (the co-founder), with three young workers in the heart of Paris, 10 minutes away from the office, next to Gare de l’Est (eastern train station). A private room, a big living room and, above all, three flatmates, always smiling and available to go out and share some meals ! All meals at home will be offered and of course, you will have the opportunity to eat when you join us for an event.

Our favorite quote : “Bread swells by taking shape of the baker’s paulm. Bringing it to ones mouth is like shaking hands with the one who kneaded it.”

Erri De Luca

Today at Monbanquet, we are about twenty young (and less young) people ready to do everything it takes to promote our banquets (dinners) and craftsmen who work with us. From Grenoble, Bordeaux, Orleans: we all come from different horizons, but we are driven by the same ambitions : promote local know-how and flood events with our local products ! We are all foodies and sportsmen (it goes along), we work together so that every event organizer can treat his guests. Bicycle races, wine tasting or “apéro” (aperitif) on the docks : all occasions are good to get together outside work.
