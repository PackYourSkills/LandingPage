Mission

We have a mission

Our mission is to enable everyone to get involved in projects with positive impact on society, by providing their skills during a trip.

Concept

Here is our plan

Pack Your Skills brings together a Packer and a Crew for the duration of the mission.

PACKERS are travelers who wish to be involved in local projects during their trip using their skills in exchange for room and accommodation.

CREWS are teams from local initiatives (startups or charities) whose primary mission is to solve a societal issue and who need key skills to develop their activity.

About Us

Pack Your Skills is a web platform that gives the opportunity to travelers to board on projects (social business or charity), join their team for the duration of the mission, using their skills in exchange for room and accommodation.

Want to give a hand ?

Become a Pack Your Skills’s Ambassador ! Seek startups or charities with positive impact during your trip and chat with them to help us understand their needs better.
