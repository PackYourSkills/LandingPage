The mission you will help us in !

Jimini’s was created in 2012 to democratize insects’ consumption in meals

Insects are good for the health (they contain proteins, B12 vitamin, omega 3 and 6, iron, calcium) and the environment (their breeding needs less water, space or food than other species like cow, pig or chicken).
We want to progressively integrate insects into people’s daily food so we create and develop delicious products to make people discover and appreciate the best of edible insects. We sell different types of insects (worms, crickets…), under the form of entire insects as appetizers or flour in snacks.
