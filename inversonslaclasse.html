Build the visual identity of our social network for teachers

The mission you will help us in !

Inversons la classe ! (which means “let’s reverse school class”) was created in 2014 to foster the success of all pupils and students in the 21st century.

We want to fight for equality and egalitarian school by giving accessibility and visibility to new educative and digital methods and tools. Our challenge is to use the modernity and technology to improve learning and teaching. Our existence is based on two things :
-Innovative teaching methods exist but don’t spread to all teachers
-Teaching methods are given to the teachers through top-down instructions; we believe that a peer-to-peer teaching of new methods would be more efficient; that’s why we are creating a social network for teachers
Our wish is to give a pedagogic meaning to the digital education and fight against school inequalities

We need a ToChange

We need a graphic designer to build our visual identity for our “professor social network”

We need a graphic designer to build our visual identity for our “professor social network”.
This social network will be used to shared all information between teachers but it will also be available for kids. We will have a part as a forum and a kind of Youtube-like, but without publicity and with user’s data protection. This will have an impact on thousands of teachers and millions of students.
This project is central for us. We have been working on this for more than 2 years with a professor as project manager. Our talented team is working on a prototype with web developers from a digital school named “Ecole 42”. You will need to speak either French or English.

Where you will work

“The Cargo” is a fantastic place all dedicated to young and creative businesses in relation with digital issues. All of this is part of a big project launched by the city of Paris in response to the great social innovation movement happening in Paris.
