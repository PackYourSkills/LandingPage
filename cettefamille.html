The mission you will help us in !

CetteFamille was created in June 2016 to propose a warm and intergenerational alternative to retirement homes which allow to build social link and which is in phase with the budget of seniors.

CetteFamille is a web platform whose aim is to bring together host families and seniors with a loss of autonomy who can no longer live alone. The aim of the start-up is clear: to boost this sector to emphase this solution and create vocations in families to host and to offer a reassuring solution for the elders.
