Our mission

Sensecube was created in 2009 to help social entrepreneurs to develop theirs projects solving social or environmental subjects. Sensecube’s goal is to support positive, economic and social change.

Sensecube it's a platform which aim is to work with young projects, help them on the way to succeed and solve problems durably.

We need a graphic designer

to update and modernize our visual identity for the SwitchUp Challenge. What’s that ?

It’s a program that supports and rewards the startup who use digital tools to answer social or environmental 21st issues.
Switch Challenge is very important in our approach. It’s become central in our commitment to sensitize as many people are possible to social entrepreneurship. We encourage people to create and we give advice to support the project’s growth.
English, French and Spanish are spoken in Sensecube. If you talk one of these languages you will exchange comfortably.

Where would you work

Our working place is located in the “Sensespace”. It’s the main office of MakeSense, a huge worldwide organization that promotes social entrepreneurship. It’s a wonderful open-space in the center of Paris (Bastille neighbourhood), full of young social entrepreneurs that launch their business : it smells passion and collaboration !

Where would you live

You will sleep in two different places, first week in Daniel’s home, you will have your own room and second week in Mathilde’s place : she has a very comfortable sofa. Both places are located in Paris’s center and more or less 30 minutes from workplace.

Le SenseCube : une équipe soudée de 13 talents. Nous construisons une équipe de personnes talentueuses, passionnées par l’innovation sociale et ayant à cœur d’utiliser leurs compétences pour résoudre les grands défis de notre société.
SenseCube et MakeSense c’est aussi une équipe internationale de plus de 70 personnes à plein temps en France, au Mexique, au Sénégal, au Liban et aux Philippines. Nous expérimentons au quotidien des nouveaux modèles organisationnels qui mettent la responsabilité, l’autonomie, la bienveillance au coeur de nos interactions. L’impact est le carburant de toutes nos actions. Nous adorons apprendre et partager, partout et tout le temps. A l’image de notre communauté, tous les défis que nous identifions se transforment en solutions construites collectivement !
