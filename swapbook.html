Create videos to promote the benefits of the mobile application.

The mission you will help us in !

Swapbook was created in May, 2016 to encourage equal opportunities. We want to improve students’ life conditions and enable them to succeed their studies at lower cost.

Swapbook is an easy-to-use mobile application that enables students to resell their books to each other, directly within campus.

We need a videographer

record and edit videos to promote the advantages of the mobile application, based on scenarios written by the team.

You will have to shoot the envisaged images and then compile them according to the provided scenarios.
You will need to speak either French or English to complete your task, but you must know that 2 people in the team only speak French so there will be conversations between some members in French only.

Where you will work

We work in a coworking space called “PSL (Paris Sciences et Lettres) Lab”, with other startups. It is located in the 5th district, on Rue Mouffetard, very famous for its numerous bars, highly frequented by students at night. We also do photo shootings every week in a square nearby so it will be a good opportunity to visit this part of Paris.

Where you will live

You will sleep in a private room in an apartment in Paris’s closeby city Neuilly-sur-Seine. It is Laure (the co-founder)’s parents place. You will go to the workplace by subway.
There is a cat in the apartment (just saying, in case you’re allergic or scared).
Concerning food, you can help yourself in the fridge for every meal when you’re at home. I am just not that much of a cook so I don’t promise I will cook meals.

An opportunity with Pack Your Skills is not a job with a salary, this is a volunteer assignment with social entrepreneurs where you get accommodation in exchange for your help. Airfare is not included.
